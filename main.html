Meeting Host Manager

Companies can now host meetings via BAE(been an email). This virtual agenda will help reduce the number of pointless meetings and engage more with employees.

Employee Profile

Employees can see their upcoming meeting's agenda, previous meetings with notes and transcription, and their tasks.

Zoom in on Meetings page

Before the meeting employees can vote up or down on agenda items. If enough people vote it down, it will go negative and then be pushed into the "BAE" list and will be sent out as an email. You can also create tasks per agenda item and assign them to attendees. Now, everyone talks about what is important and there is a clear outcome from every meeting.
